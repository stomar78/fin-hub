# Pricing Page — Visual & UX Design

- **Purpose**
  Communicate options clearly, differentiate free vs vetted services, and drive conversions.

## 1. Hero (Pricing Overview)
- **Background**: Animated royal→sea-blue gradient (#0046FF → #00BFFF → #001A66), glass-blur overlay, floating currency/chart icons.
- **Content**: H1 “Choose the Valuation Plan That Fits Your Goals”, subtitle, CTA “Compare Plans”.

## 2. Pricing Tiers
- **Layout**: Two plan cards (desktop side-by-side; mobile stacked).
- **Free Instant Valuation**
  - Price: $0 / forever
  - Features: AI valuation (DCF & Multiples), instant summary, email delivery, confidence metric
  - CTA: “Start Free Valuation” (blue gradient)
- **Vetted Analyst Valuation**
  - Price: $1499 / one-time
  - Features: IVS-compliant report, analyst-verified model, sensitivity/scenario charts, investor presentation (PDF/Excel), optional consultation
  - CTA: “Get Certified Report” (blue gradient)
- **Interactions**: Card scale on hover; premium card pulsing outline (sky-400→blue-700)

## 3. Comparison Table
- **Background**: Soft blue tint (#F5F9FF)
- **Features**: model parity, report download, IVS compliance, analyst review, model depth, charts, consultation, delivery time (Instant vs 3–5 days)
- **UX**: Column highlight on hover; sticky “Compare Plans” on mobile

## 4. Payment & Security Assurance
- **Visual**: White→light sky-blue gradient
- **Badges**: SSL Secure • PCI DSS • 3D Secure
- **Logos**: Visa, MasterCard, Stripe; shimmer sequentially
- **Copy**: “Secure, Transparent, and Global Payment Gateway”

## 5. FAQ Accordion
- **Items**: Free accuracy, IVS compliance, upgrade path, refunds
- **Motion**: Smooth slide; first item auto-expands

## 6. End CTA
- **Background**: Deep-blue animated gradient with moving beams
- **Content**: “Unlock a Transparent, AI-Driven Valuation Experience.” CTA “Start Free Valuation”

## 7. Footer
- Consistent global footer with pricing policy link.

## SEO & Accessibility
- Keywords: valuation pricing plans, certified valuation cost, AI valuation price
- Product/Offer schema; responsive table semantics
