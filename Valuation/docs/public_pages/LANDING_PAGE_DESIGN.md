# Landing Page — Visual & UX Design

- **Purpose**
  Create a premium first impression that communicates trust, innovation, and AI precision.

## 1. Hero Section (Above the Fold)
- **Background**
  - Animated sea-blue gradient: #00AEEF → #0046FF → #001F8A
  - SVG wave + dot patterns with subtle vertical motion
  - Cyan/indigo transparent overlays for depth
  - Floating glow particles (Framer Motion), 15–20 nodes
- **Layout**
  - Center-aligned headline + subtext + dual CTAs
  - Continuous hue shift every 8–10s
- **Content**
  - H1: “AI-Driven Business & Asset Valuation Portal”
  - Subtext: “Experience next-gen valuation precision using QuantLib, deep-learning analytics, and real-time global market datasets.”
  - Primary CTA: “Get Free Valuation” (white button, ripple hover)
  - Secondary CTA: “Explore Vetted Reports” (transparent, gradient-fill on hover)
- **Interactions**
  - Parallax background, button lift/shadow, particle loops

## 2. About Section
- **Visual**: White background, soft shadow divider from hero
- **Layout**: 3-card grid (stack on mobile)
- **Cards**
  - IVS-Compliant: shield+doc icon, compliance text
  - Data Integration: API/network icon, real-time accuracy
  - AI-Enhanced Accuracy: brain-chip icon, predictive + quant models
- **Hover**: lift + shadow glow

## 3. How It Works Section
- **Visual**: Gradient white → pale blue (#F8FAFF → #DCEBFF)
- **Layout**: 4 horizontal step cards with circular badges and connecting timeline
- **Steps**
  - Input Details — manual/CSV upload
  - AI Calculation — QuantLib + neural models
  - Instant Report — valuation summary in seconds
  - Analyst Review — IVS-certified upgrade
- **Animation**: step-by-step fade/scale on scroll

## 4. Pricing Section
- **Visual**: Pure white
- **Layout**: Two large cards, center-aligned; vertical stack on mobile
- **Content**
  - Title: “Choose Your Valuation Plan”
  - Free: $0 • quick AI estimate • email delivery
  - Vetted: $1499 • IVS compliance • detailed charts • investor-ready
  - CTAs on both cards, gradient pulse on hover

## 5. CTA Strip
- **Visual**: Deep sea-blue gradient (#003CBA → #001A66) with moving light waves
- **Content**
  - Heading: “Transform Your Valuation Workflow.”
  - Subtext: Automation • Compliance • Accuracy
  - Button: “Start Free Valuation” (white, blue shadow bloom)
- **Motion**: Infinite slow gradient + light ripple

## 6. Footer
- **Visual**: White background; thin gray top border
- **Content**: “© 2025 Epiidosis Valuation Portal • Powered by QuantLib & AI Precision.”
  - Links: Privacy • Terms • Contact • Social icons (muted slate)

## SEO & Accessibility
- Keywords: AI valuation platform, business valuation software, IVS-compliant valuation
- Semantics: h1/h2 hierarchy, ARIA on interactive elements
- Performance: lazy animations, optimized SVGs
