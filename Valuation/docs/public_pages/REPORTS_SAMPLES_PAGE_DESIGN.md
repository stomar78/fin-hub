# Reports & Samples Page — Visual & UX Design

- **Purpose**
  Showcase report quality, structure, and compliance; enable preview/download and drive conversions.

## 1. Hero Banner
- **Background**: Sea-blue gradient (#0033A0 → #00BFFF → #0099FF) with glass reflection overlay; floating PDF/chart/certificate icons.
- **Layout**: Left headline/subtext/CTAs; right animated report cover turning pages.
- **Content**: H1 “See What a Certified Valuation Report Looks Like”; CTAs “Download Sample Report” and “View Interactive Preview”.

## 2. Interactive Report Preview
- **Visual**: White background; center PDF.js/React-PDF viewer with soft shadow.
- **Labels**: Prepared by Epiidosis, QuantLib models, IVS/IFRS/RICS compliance.
- **Action**: Footer button “Get Your Own Valuation Report”.

## 3. Report Components Breakdown
- **Visual**: Alternating white and light gradient stripes (#F8FAFF → #E6F2FF).
- **Cards**:
  - Executive Summary — purpose, scope, findings.
  - Methodology — DCF, Multiples, NAV; sensitivity.
  - Financial Outputs — assumptions, drivers, tables.
  - Valuation Opinion & Compliance — IVS/RICS notes.

## 4. Compliance & Accreditation
- **Visual**: Gradient→white blend; glass cards for IVS 2024, IFRS, RICS logos.
- **Copy**: Global recognition by investors, institutions, auditors.

## 5. Infographic: Valuation Accuracy Spectrum
- **Chart**: Free Instant (±15%, no analyst/cert) vs Vetted (±2%, analyst+cert) vs Corporate (±1%).

## 6. Client Testimonials
- **Carousel**: Quotes from CEO/CFO/Founder with gradient accent cards.

## 7. Final CTA
- **Background**: Deep gradient; button “Request Certified Valuation”.

## 8. Footer
- Consistent white footer with links to Reports | Compliance | Pricing | Support.

## SEO & Accessibility
- Keywords: valuation report sample, IVS compliant valuation, AI financial report example.
- Semantic headings; accessible viewer controls.
