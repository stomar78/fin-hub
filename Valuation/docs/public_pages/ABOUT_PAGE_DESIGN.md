# About Page — Visual & UX Design

- **Purpose**
  Reinforce credibility, compliance, and expertise; explain why users should trust the platform.

## 1. Hero Banner
- **Background**: Deep sea-blue gradient (#002B7F → #004FFF → #0096FF), animated contour patterns, subtle particles.
- **Layout**: Split — left text, right abstract illustration (3D holographic balance scale).
- **Content**: H1 “Building Global Trust Through AI-Powered Valuation Precision”, subtitle, CTA “Learn About Our Process”.

## 2. Our Methodology
- **Visual**: White background, three horizontal cards (stack on mobile), blue highlights.
- **Cards**:
  - DCF: cash flow discounting overview.
  - Market Multiples: global comparable benchmarking.
  - QuantLib Models: robust mathematical pricing.
- **Animation**: left→right stagger fade; hover border glow.

## 3. Compliance & Standards
- **Background**: Gradient (#F8FAFF → #E3EEFF) with white center.
- **Logos**: IVS 2024, IFRS, RICS in rounded glass cards.
- **Copy**: Alignment to global frameworks, audit-ready outputs.

## 4. Team & Experts
- **Layout**: 2×2 grid (stack on mobile).
- **Cards**: portrait, name, role, short bio; gradient borders and hover depth.

## 5. Testimonial (Optional)
- **Visual**: Gradient background; slider of client quotes.

## 6. CTA
- **Content**: “Get Your Certified Valuation Report Today” + button.

## 7. Footer
- Consistent global footer (white, top border, centered text).

## SEO & Accessibility
- Keywords: AI valuation compliance, IVS valuation experts, QuantLib.
- Semantics and alt/aria; lazy animated assets.
