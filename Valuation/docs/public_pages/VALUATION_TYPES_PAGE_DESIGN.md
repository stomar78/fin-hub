# Valuation Types Page — Visual & UX Design

- **Purpose**
  Educate users on Business, Real Estate, Startup, and Asset & Machinery valuations; drive leads to start estimates.

## 1. Hero Banner
- **Background**: Deep gradient (#0033A0 → #007BFF → #00CFFF); animated curves, overlay grid.
- **Content**: H1 “Valuation Types We Offer”, subtext, CTA “Get Your Free Estimate”.

## 2. Business Valuation
- **Visual**: White background with faint analytic grid.
- **Layout**: Two columns — text & icons; dashboard illustration.
- **Copy**: DCF, EBITDA multiples, sector benchmarks; use cases: M&A, fundraising.
- **Chart**: Mini line chart (EV vs CF Growth) animates on scroll.
- **CTA**: “Request Business Valuation Report”.

## 3. Real Estate Valuation
- **Visual**: Blue gradient with white diagonal divider; isometric skyline, parallax.
- **Copy**: Income approach, comps, cap rate; location & lease dynamics.
- **Feature List**: Comps, cost data, rental yield.
- **CTA**: “Explore Property Valuation”.

## 4. Startup Valuation
- **Visual**: White with light cyan accents.
- **Copy**: Growth projections, risk models; Berkus, Risk Factor, Scorecard.
- **KPI**: Animated counter “Pre-money Range: $2.1M–$3.4M”.
- **CTA**: “Get Your Startup Valued”.

## 5. Asset & Machinery Valuation
- **Visual**: Deep gradient with blueprint/gears overlay; subtle rotation.
- **Copy**: Depreciation modeling, market comparison, cost reconstruction.
- **Use cases**: Transfer audits, insurance, liquidation.
- **CTA**: “Get Machinery Report”.

## 6. Comparative Methodology
- **Visual**: White with translucent line chart overlay.
- **Columns**: DCF, Market Multiples, NAV — 3-line summaries + icons.

## 7. Global CTA
- **Visual**: Vibrant gradient (#0055FF → #00C4FF).
- **Content**: “Discover the True Value of What You Own.” CTA “Start Your Free Valuation Now”.

## 8. Footer
- Consistent global footer with links.

## SEO & Accessibility
- Keywords: business valuation services, real estate appraisal, startup valuation calculator, machinery valuation tool.
- Proper headings, alt text; performant animations.
