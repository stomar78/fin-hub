# Contact / Support Page — Visual & UX Design

- **Purpose**
  Provide a high-trust, low-friction channel to reach valuation experts and support.

## 1. Hero Banner
- **Background**: Royal→sea-blue gradient (#002B7F → #00BFFF), animated wave/particles.
- **Layout**: Left text + CTAs; right support illustration.
- **Content**: H1 “We’re Here to Help You Every Step of the Way”, subtext, CTAs “Talk to a Valuation Advisor” and “Explore FAQs”.

## 2. Contact Form
- **Layout**: Two columns (form / details); centered container with rounded shadow.
- **Fields**: Name, Email, Subject, Message, Attachment (optional).
- **Submit**: Gradient button; success toast, 24h SLA note; sends to support mailbox + CRM.

## 3. Quick Contact Cards
- **Cards**: Email Support, Phone Support, Office Location; glassmorphism, hover lift.
- **Details**: support@epiidosisvaluation.com • +971 4 123 5678 • Dubai Silicon Oasis, UAE.

## 4. Map & Location
- **Embed**: Google Maps with brand-styled marker.
- **Overlay**: “Visit Our Office” + CTA “Book a Meeting”.

## 5. Live Chat Widget
- **Button**: Floating gradient chat icon; opens drawer.
- **Assistant**: “Ava – Epiidosis Support Assistant”. Options: report update, talk to analyst, troubleshooting.

## 6. FAQ Teaser
- **Accordion**: 3 common questions; link to full FAQ section on Pricing page.

## 7. Final CTA
- **Background**: Deep gradient; “Let’s Begin Your Valuation Journey” + “Start Free Valuation”.

## 8. Footer
- Consistent global footer with Terms | Privacy | Support | Contact.

## SEO & Accessibility
- Keywords: valuation advisor contact, AI valuation support, get valuation help.
- Form labels, aria-live for errors, keyboard-accessible chat.
