# QA Checklist (IVS 2024 Aligned)

- Purpose and standard of value documented (IVS)
- Valuation date and currency set and consistent
- Data sources identified and time-stamped; rate limits noted
- Assumptions register filled, with rationale and owner
- WACC inputs: rf, beta, ERP, CRP, size/specific; sources recorded
- DCF guardrails: terminal g within reasonable bounds; sensitivity grid generated
- Multiples: comps selection rationale; outlier treatment; bands p25–p75
- Reconciliation: DCF vs Multiples gap within thresholds or explained
- Limitations and reliance statements included
- Free PDF watermark and disclaimers present
- Audit log captures key actions
