# Golden Test Cases

## Case 1: SaaS Growth SME (INR)
- Inputs: revenue 15M INR, EBITDA 18%, growth 25%, rf=7.2%, ERP=5.5%, CRP=2.0%
- Expected: WACC ~ 12–14%; DCF vs Multiples within 15%

## Case 2: Stable Manufacturing (USD)
- Inputs: margin 12%, growth 4%, rf=4.0%, ERP=5.0%
- Expected: WACC ~ 9–10%; TV method Gordon with g=2.5%

## Case 3: Negative EBITDA Startup
- Use revenue multiple only; DCF with negative year-1 FCF allowed; cap exit multiple.
