# Account, Company, and Financial Data Workflow

- **Account Types**: Individual, Company, Valuation Partner, Admin.
- **Registration**: email + OTP, phone OTP, business info for companies.
- **Company Profile**: basics, model, management, operations, uploads.
- **Financials**: historical (3y), projections (3–5y), currency, auditor.
- **Uploads**: XLSX/CSV/PDF, OCR, AI validation and ratios.
- **Integrations**: QuickBooks, Xero, Zoho, Odoo, Tally, FreshBooks.
- **Normalization**: Unified Financial Data Schema (UFDS), FX conversion.
- **AI Pre-Processing**: ratios, anomaly detection, readiness score.
- **Valuation**: instant AI (DCF/Multiples) + certified analyst workflow.
- **Reporting**: instant PDF (watermarked) and certified PDF (IVS/IFRS).
- **Notifications**: progress tracker, download links, revisions.
- **Security**: OAuth2, AES-256 token storage, GDPR.
