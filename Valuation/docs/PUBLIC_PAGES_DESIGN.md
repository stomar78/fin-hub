## Design System Notes
- Typography: Raleway (headings), Inter (body), IBM Plex Mono (numbers).
- Colors: Royal→Sea Blue gradients; 70% white layout; slate neutrals.
- Motion: Framer Motion fade/slide; parallax hero; hover lift/glow.
- Accessibility: WCAG 2.1 AA; semantic HTML; ARIA; alt text.
